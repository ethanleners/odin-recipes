Cake

Description

This easy vanilla cake is a decadant go-to for all of your celebratory needs.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Good vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Love
  • Kindness

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix wet ingredients.
  3. In a different bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  4. Combine wet and dry and mix for two minutes.
  5. Pour batter into a prepared cake pan.
  6. Bake for 25-30 mintues or until toothpick comes out clean.
