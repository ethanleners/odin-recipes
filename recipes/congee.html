Congee

Description

Warm and hearty breakfast for any meal. This rice porridge will touch your soul.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Carrots
  • Choice of protein
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Add rice and stock to a pot and cover.
  2. Heat over medium.
  3. Chop carrots, scallions, and protein.
  4. Add chopped ingredients to the pot.
  5. Bring to a boil and reduce heat.
  6. Cook until desired consistency has been reached.
