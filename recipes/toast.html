Toast

Description

Who can say no to toast?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Optional: Cinnamon sugar

Steps

  1. Start with pre-softened butter.
  2. Put desired number of slices of bread into the toaster.
  3. When adequate toasting has occured, remove.
  4. Quickly spread soft butter onto the hot toast.
  5. If using, liberally sprinkle cinnamon sugar onto the buttery toast.
  6. Enjoy with tea.
